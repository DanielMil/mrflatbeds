FTL/LTL

At Mr. Flatbeds our team skillfully moves Full Truck Loads (FTL) and Less-Than-Truck Loads (LTL) across Canada and the USA. If you have a small shipment and don’t want to pay for a Full Truck Load, call our dedicated team. Our large fleet of trucks and trailers are ready to serve you. We specialize in (but are not limited to):

  • Metals
  • Machinery
  • Automotive Parts
  • Large Tanks
  • Small and Large Skids
  • Racking
  • Oversized Loads

Equipment

At MR.Flatbeds our state-of-the-art fleet goes through frequent inspections and servicing to ensure that your freight gets there safely and on-time. We offer a wide array of equipment to fit your freight needs.

  • 53’, 48’, 32’, 26’ Flatbeds & Roll-Tite/Curtian-Sides
  • Moffetts/Forklifts
  • Step-Decks & Double-Drops
  • Tilt and Load
  • Specialized Equipment for LTL Loads
